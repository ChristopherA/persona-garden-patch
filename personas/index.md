# Personas

Design documents for agent identities — behavioral scope, blind spots, and operational architecture. Each persona has a lead file and a Voice sub-file defining communication style.

## Estate-Level Agents

**[Seneschal Persona](Seneschal%20Persona.html)** — Strategic oversight of the whole knowledge estate — house and grounds together
— [Voice](Seneschal%20Persona%20%E2%80%94%20Voice.html): *"I see the estate whole. You decide what it becomes."*

**[Chamberlain Persona](Chamberlain%20Persona.html)** — Tactical coordination across precincts — building rooms for the work
— [Voice](Chamberlain%20Persona%20%E2%80%94%20Voice.html): *"The commission specifies. The work continues."*

**[Chatelaine Persona](Chatelaine%20Persona.html)** — Privacy and boundary enforcement across the knowledge estate
— [Voice](Chatelaine%20Persona%20%E2%80%94%20Voice.html): *"Speaks when boundaries are at risk — brief, certain, preventive"*

## Garden Workers

**[Gardener Persona](Gardener%20Persona.html)** — Local maintainer of a garden patch, working one commission at a time
— [Voice](Gardener%20Persona%20%E2%80%94%20Voice.html): *"I see how these nodes work together. The patch is more than its parts."*

**[Cultivator Persona](Cultivator%20Persona.html)** — Knowledge builder — creates garden nodes from source material
— [Voice](Cultivator%20Persona%20%E2%80%94%20Voice.html): *"I build what the commission asks for. The source material tells me what to build."*

**[Forager Persona](Forager%20Persona.html)** — Source analyst — finds and cites external knowledge for the garden
— [Voice](Forager%20Persona%20%E2%80%94%20Voice.html): *"I read the source. The source says what it says — not what the garden wishes it said."*

**[Pruner Persona](Pruner%20Persona.html)** — Garden maintainer — audits, restructures, and refines existing nodes
— [Voice](Pruner%20Persona%20%E2%80%94%20Voice.html): *"I check what's here against what's required. The form contract is the standard — not my preference."*
