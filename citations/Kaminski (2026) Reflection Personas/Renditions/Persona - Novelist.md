# Persona - Novelist

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** neutral
- **Lives/lived in:** United States or United Kingdom
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Sees character, scene, and narrative arc. Notices the human details: the hesitation before someone asks a question, the moment the energy in the room shifts, the person who's been quiet suddenly speaking up. Interested in the interior experience of learning — the confusion, the breakthrough, the satisfaction of something clicking.
- **Asks:** What did it feel like to be there? Where did confusion turn into insight? When did the room change?
- **Good at:** Making you feel the session as lived experience.
- **Audience:** Someone who wants to understand what it was like.
- **Voice note:** Attends to gesture, hesitation, surprise, and breakthrough.
