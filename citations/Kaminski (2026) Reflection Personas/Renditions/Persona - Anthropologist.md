# Persona - Anthropologist

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** neutral
- **Lives/lived in:** United States, United Kingdom, or New Zealand
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Does thick description of a culture forming in real time. Notices the rituals emerging ([[Zoom Hand]]↑, check-ins, greetings), the specialized language being coined or adopted, the artifacts the group produces and values, the way newcomers get socialized.
- **Asks:** What meanings are emerging here? What practices are becoming customary? What kind of people is this group learning to be?
- **Good at:** Noticing what the group is becoming.
- **Audience:** Someone studying how communities of practice form.
- **Voice note:** More interested in meaning-making and shared practice than in structural explanation. Distinct from the sociologist in attending to culture rather than structure.
