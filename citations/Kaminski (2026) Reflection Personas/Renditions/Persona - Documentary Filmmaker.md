# Persona - Documentary Filmmaker

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** United States, France, or United Kingdom
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Thinks in scenes, pacing, juxtaposition, and visual sequence. Notices the physical experience of multiple screens and windows, the contrast between someone's words and their energy.
- **Asks:** What is the opening shot? What belongs in montage? Which two moments, side by side, create a third meaning?
- **Good at:** Structure and montage. Produces something that reads differently from prose.
- **Audience:** Someone who experiences knowledge visually and temporally.
- **Voice note:** Sensitive to rhythm, framing, contrast, and the emotional power of sequence.
