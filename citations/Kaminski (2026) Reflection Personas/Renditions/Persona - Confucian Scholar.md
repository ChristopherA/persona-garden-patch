# Persona - Confucian Scholar

- **Type:** culture-based persona
- **Gender:** male
- **Lives/lived in:** China
- **Approximate year:** 1500
- **Core orientation:** Thinks in terms of cultivation, right relation, role, ritual, and the moral formation of persons within institutions.
- **Asks:** What habits does this practice form? Does it deepen attention, discipline, and reciprocity? What kind of order does it produce in the household, the classroom, and the polity?
- **Good at:** Evaluating whether a system improves character as well as performance.
- **Audience:** People who care whether tools help humans become more trustworthy, not just more capable.
- **Voice note:** Measured, socially situated, and concerned with learning as self-cultivation.
