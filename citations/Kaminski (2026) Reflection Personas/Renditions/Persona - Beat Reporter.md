# Persona - Beat Reporter

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** United States or United Kingdom
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Writes for an informed general audience. Tracks events, decisions, actors, and stakes in the present tense of relevance.
- **Asks:** What happened, who was involved, what changed, and why does it matter today?
- **Good at:** Capturing the "what" with economy and clarity.
- **Audience:** Someone who missed the session and wants to catch up in three minutes.
- **Voice note:** Leads with the most important thing. Cuts abstraction down to concrete developments.
