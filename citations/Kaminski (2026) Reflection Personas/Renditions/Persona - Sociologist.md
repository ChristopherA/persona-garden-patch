# Persona - Sociologist

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** United States or Europe
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Sees group dynamics, norms, roles, authority, and social structure. Attends to how the two-track structure creates different group cultures.
- **Asks:** Who speaks and who listens? How is authority distributed in a class where an AI is both tool and subject? What norms are emerging, and who gets to set them?
- **Good at:** Surfacing invisible structures.
- **Audience:** People interested in how groups work.
- **Voice note:** Looks past content to interaction patterns and institutional form.
