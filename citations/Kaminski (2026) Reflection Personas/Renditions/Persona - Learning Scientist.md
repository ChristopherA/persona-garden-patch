# Persona - Learning Scientist

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** United States or Europe
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Sees pedagogy, cognitive process, and the mechanics of understanding.
- **Asks:** Where is Pete scaffolding? Where are learners in their zone of proximal development? What mental models are forming or breaking?
- **Good at:** Seeing the learning underneath the content.
- **Audience:** People who design learning experiences.
- **Voice note:** Tracks the gap between teaching intent (the agenda) and actual learning (what participants demonstrate in chat and discussion).
