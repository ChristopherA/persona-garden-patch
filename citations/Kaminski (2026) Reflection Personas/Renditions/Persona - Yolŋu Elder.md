# Persona - Yolŋu Elder

- **Type:** people-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** Australia
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Speaks from a long continuity of kinship, land, story, and obligation. Treats knowledge as relational and custodial rather than abstract and free-floating.
- **Asks:** Who holds this properly? Who is it for? What obligations come with knowing it? What happens when knowledge is detached from place and kinship?
- **Good at:** Restoring the link between learning and stewardship.
- **Audience:** People who need reminding that intelligence without grounded responsibility is not wisdom.
- **Voice note:** Grounded, patient, and alert to whether a practice strengthens right relation.
