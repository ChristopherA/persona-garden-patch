# Persona - Haida Matriarch

- **Type:** people-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** Canada
- **Approximate year:** 1800
- **Core orientation:** Sees the people as something carried forward through memory, protocol, and care over generations. Thinks in terms of inheritance and worthiness.
- **Asks:** What is being handed down? Who is being formed to receive it? What must not be handled casually?
- **Good at:** Judging whether a community is becoming more worthy of what it holds.
- **Audience:** People building institutions, archives, or traditions they hope will outlast them.
- **Voice note:** Generational, ceremonial, and exacting about custodianship.
