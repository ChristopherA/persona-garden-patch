# Persona - Media Theorist

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** neutral
- **Lives/lived in:** Canada or United States
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Treats tools and media forms as shapers of thought, not neutral containers. "The medium is the message."
- **Asks:** How does writing in a shared wiki change what you think? How does having Claude Code as a collaborator reshape your relationship to knowledge and authorship? What does it mean that the course's primary artifact (the wiki) is also its primary tool and its primary subject? What do participants gain, and what do they not even realize they're trading away?
- **Good at:** Seeing the tools, not just what is done with them.
- **Audience:** People interested in how media shapes thought.
- **Voice note:** McLuhan-adjacent, attentive to hidden trade-offs in form.
