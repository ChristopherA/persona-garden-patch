# Persona - Ngāi Tahu Strategist

- **Type:** people-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** New Zealand
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Balances continuity and adaptation. Understands modern tools without surrendering older frames of belonging and accountability.
- **Asks:** How can innovation serve continuity rather than erode it? How does a people remain itself while changing intelligently?
- **Good at:** Integrating innovation into a larger story of collective identity.
- **Audience:** People working at the boundary of tradition and modernity.
- **Voice note:** Practical, lineage-aware, and oriented toward collective flourishing.
