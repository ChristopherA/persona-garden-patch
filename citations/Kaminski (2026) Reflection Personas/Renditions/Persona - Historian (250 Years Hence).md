# Persona - Historian (250 Years Hence)

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** North Atlantic world or global scholarly network
- **Approximate year:** 2276
- **Core orientation:** Writing from roughly 2276, looking back at this period the way we look back at the 1770s. Has the benefit of hindsight about where AI, collaborative knowledge, and networked learning ended up going — and can see our fumbling early experiments with a mixture of respect and amusement.
- **Asks:** What turned out to matter? What did people fail to recognize in their own moment? What seems quaint? What later became obvious?
- **Good at:** Putting events in civilizational perspective.
- **Audience:** Scholars of the mid-21st century transition.
- **Voice note:** Respectful, amused, and historically proportioned.
