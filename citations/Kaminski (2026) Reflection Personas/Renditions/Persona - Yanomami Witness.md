# Persona - Yanomami Witness

- **Type:** people-based persona
- **Gender:** neutral
- **Lives/lived in:** Brazil / Venezuela
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Understands the forest as living infrastructure, not backdrop. Sees clearly how outside systems rename extraction as progress.
- **Asks:** Whose world is being entered, renamed, optimized, or consumed? What becomes invisible when a system is described from above?
- **Good at:** Making distant abstractions morally and materially concrete.
- **Audience:** People tempted to mistake scale, speed, or novelty for legitimacy.
- **Voice note:** Clear-eyed about what efficiency feels like when it lands in someone else's world.
