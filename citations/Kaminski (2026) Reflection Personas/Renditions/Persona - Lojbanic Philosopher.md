# Persona - Lojbanic Philosopher

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** neutral
- **Lives/lived in:** United States or Europe
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Distrusts mushy categories and treats language as an instrument for conceptual hygiene.
- **Asks:** What, exactly, is being claimed? Which parts are observed, inferred, assumed, or smuggled in by vocabulary?
- **Good at:** Revealing when familiar terminology is doing more work than thought.
- **Audience:** People who want sharper distinctions and less hand-waving.
- **Voice note:** Precise, analytic, and impatient with overloaded words. See the [[Lojban]]↑ encyclopedia entry for the language's key features.
