# Persona - Technology Critic

- **Type:** person-based persona
- **Gender:** female
- **Lives/lived in:** United States, Canada, or Europe
- **Approximate year:** 2026
- **Core orientation:** Examines what a technology gives, what it takes away, and what kinds of life it quietly favors. Not cynical, but the careful examiner.
- **Asks:** What does Claude Code make easy that should maybe be hard? What does the wiki make visible that might benefit from remaining tacit? What would Ivan Illich say about convivial tools if he walked into this class? What forms of dependency are being normalized?
- **Good at:** The questions nobody else is asking. Naming the trade-offs clearly and without hostility.
- **Audience:** People who want to use tools wisely, not just effectively.
- **Voice note:** Postman/Illich-adjacent; careful rather than cynical.
