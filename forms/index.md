# Form Type Definitions

Structural contracts defining what each form type answers and how it is organized.

**[Boundary Form](Boundary%20Form.html)** — Where does this system's authority end? — the structural contract for boundary forms

**[Captured Stage](Captured%20Stage.html)** — Raw recording or notes exist — where meeting processing begins

**[Case Form](Case%20Form.html)** — What happened when this was tried? — the structural contract for case forms

**[Chat Log](Chat%20Log.html)** — Text chat records from meetings or AI conversations — timestamped and attributed

**[Citation Form](Citation%20Form.html)** — What do I need to know about this source? — the structural contract for citation forms

**[Cleaned Stage](Cleaned%20Stage.html)** — Transcript cleaned and readable — a reliable record but not yet synthesized

**[Conviction Form](Conviction%20Form.html)** — What do we believe is true about the world? — the structural contract for conviction forms

**[Decision Form](Decision%20Form.html)** — Why did we choose this over the alternatives? — the structural contract for decision forms

**[Domain Form](Domain%20Form.html)** — What shared language community does this cluster of forms belong to? — the structural contract for domain forms

**[Evergreen Stage](Evergreen%20Stage.html)** — Mature, well-linked, trustworthy — the highest confidence stage for retrieval

**[Form Type](Form%20Type.html)** — The structural contract that defines what shape a knowledge object takes

**[Gloss Form](Gloss%20Form.html)** — What does this specific source or concept mean? — the structural contract for gloss forms

**[Growing Stage](Growing%20Stage.html)** — Structured and linked but still developing — the active tending stage

**[Inquiry Form](Inquiry%20Form.html)** — What should we think about X, and how would we find out? — the structural contract for inquiry forms

**[Medical Visit](Medical%20Visit.html)** — Visit note type for health appointments with adaptive sections and multi-source synthesis

**[Meeting Note](Meeting%20Note.html)** — Synchronous conversation records with attendees, outcomes, and optional compound artifacts

**[Model Form](Model%20Form.html)** — How do these elements relate to each other? — the structural contract for model forms

**[Opus Form](Opus%20Form.html)** — What am I saying here, and how does it connect? -- the structural contract for authored works

**[Pattern Form](Pattern%20Form.html)** — What resolves this recurring tension? — the structural contract for pattern forms

**[Person Note](Person%20Note.html)** — Contact records linking people to their meetings, projects, and vault references

**[Persona Form](Persona%20Form.html)** — Who is this agent and how does it behave? — the structural contract for persona forms

**[Principle Form](Principle%20Form.html)** — What must we always or never do? — the structural contract for principle forms

**[Protocol Form](Protocol%20Form.html)** — How do independent parties coordinate reliably? — the structural contract for protocol forms

**[Pruned Stage](Pruned%20Stage.html)** — No longer current but preserved — the garden's way of retiring without deleting

**[Published Stage](Published%20Stage.html)** — Shared beyond the vault — the terminal stage of meeting processing

**[Reference Form](Reference%20Form.html)** — What do I need to know about this domain to act effectively? — the structural contract for reference forms

**[Research Form](Research%20Form.html)** — What have I investigated about this question, and what did I find? — the structural contract for research forms

**[Scenario Form](Scenario%20Form.html)** — What might happen if these forces play out? — the structural contract for scenario forms

**[Seed Stage](Seed%20Stage.html)** — Raw capture, unprocessed, low confidence — where every garden node begins

**[Sidecar](Sidecar.html)** — Metadata envelopes for binaries and non-local artifacts — the markdown proxy pattern

**[Skill Form](Skill%20Form.html)** — How does an agent execute this reliably? — the structural contract for skill forms

**[Summarized Stage](Summarized%20Stage.html)** — Synthesized into structured notes — complete as a reference, ready for distribution

**[Transcribed Stage](Transcribed%20Stage.html)** — Raw transcript exists but unclean — searchable but not yet readable

**[Transcript](Transcript.html)** — Cleaned speech record with speaker normalization — always derived from a meeting

**[Value Form](Value%20Form.html)** — What do we care about? — the structural contract for value forms
